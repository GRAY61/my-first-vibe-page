Four AI Tools, One Playbook

Understand the strengths of each tool so you can pick the right one for every job.

G

Genspar

Business Automation

Genspar is purpose-built for business workflows, letting entrepreneurs automate repetitive tasks, generate structured documents, and run AI-powered processes across their operations — all without needing to code.

Workflow Automation Document Generation Business Ops

Best for entrepreneurs when…

  • Automating proposal or contract generation
  • Building repeatable AI workflows for your team
  • Structuring and routing business processes with AI
  • Integrating AI outputs into existing tools
◆

Claude

Anthropic · Deep Reasoning

Claude by Anthropic excels at nuanced, long-form tasks — digesting 100-page documents, drafting persuasive content, and reasoning through complex decisions. It's known for being careful, thorough, and honest about what it doesn't know.

Long Documents Strategic Writing Analysis Safe & Reliable

Best for entrepreneurs when…

  • Summarizing investor decks, contracts, or research
  • Writing thoughtful pitches, bios, or op-eds
  • Getting a second opinion on a business decision
  • Working through nuanced customer or HR scenarios
GPT

ChatGPT

OpenAI · Versatile Generalist

The tool that introduced most of the world to AI. ChatGPT is fast, widely integrated, and plugged into a large ecosystem of plugins and GPT apps. It's a versatile generalist that can handle everything from coding to customer support scripts.

General Purpose Plugin Ecosystem Image Generation Code

Best for entrepreneurs when…

  • Quickly drafting social media or marketing copy
  • Using pre-built GPT apps for specific niches
  • Generating images for presentations (DALL-E)
  • Writing or debugging simple scripts and automations
✦

Gemini

Google · Workspace Integration

Google's flagship AI is deeply integrated with Google Workspace — Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Slides, and Search. For entrepreneurs already living in the Google ecosystem, Gemini brings AI directly into the tools they use every day.

Google Workspace Search Grounding Multimodal Real-Time Data

Best for entrepreneurs when…

  • Drafting emails and summarizing threads in Gmail
  • Building slides and reports inside Google Docs
  • Pulling live web data into your research
  • Analyzing spreadsheets directly in Google Sheets

Entrepreneur Playbook

5 Rules for Getting Results

These principles apply no matter which AI tool you're using.

01

Give Context, Not Just Commands

Tell the AI who you are, what you're building, and who your customer is. Better context = dramatically better output.

02

Iterate, Don't Accept the First Draft

The first response is a starting point. Keep refining with follow-up prompts like "make it shorter" or "make it more persuasive."

03

Always Verify Facts

AI can hallucinate statistics, names, and citations. Cross-check anything you'll use in public-facing materials.

04

Match the Tool to the Job

Use Gemini for Workspace tasks, Claude for deep reading, ChatGPT for quick drafts, and Genspar for repeatable business workflows.

05

Build Your Prompt Library

Save your best-performing prompts in a doc. Great prompts are a business asset — treat them like one.

06

Start Small, Scale Fast

Pick one repetitive task this week and hand it to AI. Once it works, expand to the next task. Build momentum gradually.
